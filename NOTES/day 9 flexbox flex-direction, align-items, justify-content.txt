Flexbox (aka display:flex)

MAIN axis refers to main starting point of content and the direction it flows
flex-direction: row (main axis top left to top right ====>)
flex-direction: row-reverse (main axis top right to top left <====)
flex-direction: column (main axis top left to bottom left)
flex-direction: column-reverse (main axis bottom left to top left)

REFERS TO POSITION IN RELATION TO MAIN AXIS:
justify-content: 
// justify-items:

REFERS TO POSITION IN RELATION TO CROSS AXIS: 
align-items: flex-start, center, flex-end, etc.
align-content: (only affects content when/if it falls on more than one line)
